Item 2.  Properties.
At December 31, 2024, the Company operated 127 full service banking centers, 22 of which are leased facilities.  Our core banking operating markets are located within the four state region of Ohio, Indiana, Kentucky and Illinois.  First Financial's executive office is a leased facility located in Cincinnati, Ohio and we operate 54 banking centers in Ohio, three banking centers in Illinois, 59 banking centers in Indiana and 11 banking centers in Kentucky.  In addition, we operate our Commercial Finance division, responsible for our insurance lending business and franchise lending business, from a non-banking center location in Indiana.
