Item 2. Properties

Saia is headquartered in Johns Creek, Georgia and has additional general offices in Houma, Louisiana and Boise, Idaho. At December 31, 2024, Saia owned 131 service facilities, including the Houma, Louisiana general office, and leased 87 service facilities, including the Johns Creek, Georgia corporate office, the Boise, Idaho general office and the Dallas, Texas warehouse. At December 31, 2024, Saia owned 60 percent of its service facilities, accounting for 71 percent of its door capacity. As of December 31, 2024, Saia owned approximately 6,600 tractors and 26,200 trailers, inclusive of trailers acquired with finance leases.
