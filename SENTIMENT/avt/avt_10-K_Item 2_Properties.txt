Item 2. Properties
The Company’s Corporate and EC Americas headquarters are in Phoenix, Arizona. The Company owns and leases approximately 2.1 million and 3.7 million square feet of space, respectively, of which approximately 26% is in the United States. The facilities consist of warehousing, integration and value-added operations. The Company believes that the facilities are well maintained and adequate for current and future operating needs.
