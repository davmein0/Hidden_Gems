Item 2.
Properties
We operate from our main office in Cleveland, Ohio, our 37 full-service branch offices located in Ohio and Florida and our two loan production offices located in Ohio. Our branch offices are located in the Ohio counties of Cuyahoga, Lake, Lorain, Medina and Summit and in the Florida counties of Broward, Collier, Hillsborough, Lee, Palm Beach, Pasco, Pinellas and Sarasota. Our loan production offices are located in the Ohio counties of Butler and Delaware. The Company owns the building in which its home office and executive offices are located, and six other office locations. The net book value of our land, premises, equipment and software was $33.2 million at September 30, 2024, a $1.5 million decrease from September 30, 2023.
