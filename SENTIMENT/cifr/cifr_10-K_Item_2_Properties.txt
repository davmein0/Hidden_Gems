Item 2. Properties.
As of December 31, 2024, we leased all of our office space located in New York, New York, Charleston, South Carolina, and Denver, Colorado. We also lease land for our data center facilities in Odessa, Texas, Winkler County, Texas, and Cotulla, Texas.
We own land in West Texas for two of our sites.
Management believes its facilities are adequate for the Company’s near-term needs and that should it be needed, suitable additional or alternative space will be available to accommodate our operations.
