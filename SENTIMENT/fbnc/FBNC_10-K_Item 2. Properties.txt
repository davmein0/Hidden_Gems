Item 2. Properties
The main offices of the Company and the Bank are located in a building in Southern Pines, North Carolina that is owned by the Bank. The building houses corporate, accounting, and administrative facilities. The Bank’s operational departments, including accounting functions, information technology operations, loan operations, and deposit operations, are primarily housed in buildings in Greensboro, North Carolina; Dunn, North Carolina; Fletcher, North Carolina; and Troy, North Carolina, which are owned by the Bank.
