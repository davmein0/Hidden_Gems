Item 2. Properties.
Our corporate headquarters building is located in suburban Grand Rapids, Michigan. We currently have approximately 215 facilities located throughout the United States, Mexico, Canada, Spain, India, United Arab Emirates and Australia. Depending upon function and location, these facilities typically utilize office, manufacturing, and indoor and outdoor storage space.
